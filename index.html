The Tesla Model S is an all-electric five-door liftback sedan produced by Tesla, and was introduced on June 22, 2012. As of August 2020, the Model S Long Range Plus has an EPA range of 402 miles (647 km), higher than that of any other battery electric car

The Tesla Model X is a mid-size all-electric luxury crossover made by Tesla. The vehicle is notable in that it uses falcon-wing doors for passenger access.he Model X is only available as the Long Range Plus with an estimated EPA range of 371 miles (597 km) and the Performance model with an estimated EPA range of 341 miles (549 km)

The Tesla Model Y is an electric compact crossover utility vehicle (CUV) by Tesla. Tesla unveiled it in March 2019, started production at its Fremont plant in January 2020 and started deliveries on March 13, 2020.
